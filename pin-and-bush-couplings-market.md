<p><p>The Pin and Bush Couplings market is segmented into flexible and rigid types. Both types effectively transmit torque between rotating shafts, with the flexible option enhancing durability and performance in variable conditions, whereas the rigid version ensures strength and stability in high-load scenarios.</p></p>
