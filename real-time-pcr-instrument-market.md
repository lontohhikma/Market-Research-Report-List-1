<p><strong>The Real-time PCR Instrument Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One-step Real Time PCR</li><li>Two-step Real Time PCR</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The real-time PCR instrument market is primarily divided into two types: one-step and two-step real-time PCR. One-step real-time PCR combines both reverse transcription and amplification in a single tube, allowing for quicker results and reduced contamination risk. In contrast, two-step real-time PCR separates these processes, performing reverse transcription first, followed by amplification, leading to greater flexibility and optimization of each step. Both methods serve distinct research and clinical needs, driving demand in various applications.</p></p>
