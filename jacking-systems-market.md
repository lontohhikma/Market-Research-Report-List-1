<p><p>Jacking systems are critical in heavy lifting and transport operations, with market types categorized by their configuration. The H-Configuration features two parallel jacks, enhancing stability for larger loads. T-Configuration allows for versatile positioning, accommodating irregularly shaped objects. U-Configuration offers a compact design for tighter spaces, while I-Configuration provides a linear arrangement, ideal for long loads. Each configuration supports specific lifting and maneuvering needs, making them indispensable in construction, shipbuilding, and heavy machinery applications.</p></p>
