<p><p>The manganese dioxide market is categorized into three main types: Electrolytic Manganese Dioxide (EMD), Natural Manganese Dioxide (NMD), and Chemical Manganese Dioxide (CMD). EMD is primarily used in batteries and as an electrolyte in various industries. NMD is sourced from natural deposits, utilized in steel production and as a pigment. CMD is produced through chemical processes and is often employed in ceramics and fertilizers. Each type serves distinct applications based on purity, manufacturing process, and end-use requirements.</p></p>
