<p><ul><li>Thermopile Pyranometers</li><li>Photovoltaic Pyranometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pyranometer market consists primarily of two types: thermopile pyranometers and photovoltaic pyranometers. Thermopile pyranometers measure solar radiation by converting thermal energy into an electrical voltage, offering high precision and stability, making them ideal for scientific and meteorological applications. In contrast, photovoltaic pyranometers utilize a solar cell to generate a voltage proportional to incoming light, generally providing faster responses but less accuracy. Both types play crucial roles in solar energy assessment and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
