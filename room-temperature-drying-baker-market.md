<p><p>The Room Temperature Drying Baker market is divided into two main types: Horizontal and Vertical Room Temperature Drying Bakers. Horizontal Bakers feature trays arranged horizontally, allowing for uniform airflow and even drying, making them suitable for larger batches. In contrast, Vertical Bakers utilize a vertical design that optimizes space and enhances drying efficiency by minimizing moisture retention. Both types cater to various industrial applications, including food processing and pharmaceuticals, where maintaining product quality during drying is essential.</p></p>
