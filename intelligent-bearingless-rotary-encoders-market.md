<p><strong>The Intelligent Bearingless Rotary Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Rotary Encoder</li><li>Incremental Rotary Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The intelligent bearingless rotary encoders market includes two main types: absolute rotary encoders and incremental rotary encoders. Absolute rotary encoders provide a unique position value for each shaft rotation, allowing precise and absolute positioning even after power loss. In contrast, incremental rotary encoders generate pulses as the shaft rotates, enabling relative position tracking. Both types serve critical roles in automation and precision engineering, enhancing performance, reliability, and efficiency in various applications across industries.</p></p>
