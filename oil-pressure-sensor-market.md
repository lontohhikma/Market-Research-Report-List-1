<p><ul><li>Semiconductor Piezoresistive Sensor</li><li>Elastic Strain Sensor</li><li>Thick Film Pressure Sensor</li><li>Ceramic Piezoresistive Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The oil pressure sensor market includes various types of technologies. Semiconductor piezoresistive sensors utilize semiconductor materials to detect pressure changes, while elastic strain sensors measure deformation caused by pressure. Thick film pressure sensors employ a printed conductive film to sense pressure variations, offering durability and compactness. Ceramic piezoresistive sensors combine high accuracy and high-temperature tolerance, making them suitable for demanding environments. Each type serves specific applications in automotive, industrial, and aerospace sectors, driven by the need for reliable pressure monitoring.</p></p>
