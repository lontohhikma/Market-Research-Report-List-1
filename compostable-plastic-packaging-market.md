<p><p>The compostable plastic packaging market is divided into two main types: bio-based compostable plastics and synthetic-based compostable plastics. Bio-based compostable plastics are derived from renewable biomass sources, such as plants or agricultural waste, and are designed to decompose efficiently in composting environments. In contrast, synthetic-based compostable plastics are made from conventional petrochemical sources, but are engineered to break down under specific conditions. Both types aim to reduce environmental impact by offering sustainable alternatives to traditional plastics.</p></p>
