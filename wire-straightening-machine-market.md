<p><p>The wire straightening machine market primarily consists of two types: mechanical and numerically controlled. In contrast, numerically controlled machines employ advanced technology, allowing for precise adjustments and automation. This results in higher efficiency and accuracy, making them ideal for high-volume production and complex wire forms. As industries demand greater precision and speed, the numerically controlled segment is gaining significant traction.</p></p>
