<p><p>The PN junction ordinary rectifier diode market is segmented into two primary types: axial and surface-mounted. Axial diodes feature leads extending from their ends, allowing for easy integration into through-hole applications, making them popular in traditional circuit designs. Conversely, surface-mounted diodes are designed for modern electronics, offering compactness and efficiency by being soldered directly onto the circuit board. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to various design preferences and space constraints in electronic devices.</p></p>
