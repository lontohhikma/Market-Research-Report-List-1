<p><strong>The POS Receipt Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impact POS Receipt Printer</li><li>Thermal POS Receipt Printer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The POS receipt printer market is primarily divided into two types: impact and thermal printers. Impact POS receipt printers use a mechanism that strikes an inked ribbon against the paper, making them suitable for multi-part receipts and environments where durability is crucial. Conversely, thermal POS receipt printers utilize heat to produce printed images on specially coated paper, offering faster printing speeds and quieter operation. The growing emphasis on efficiency and reduced operating costs is driving the preference for thermal printers in retail and hospitality sectors.</p></p>
