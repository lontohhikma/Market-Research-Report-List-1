<p><p>The uncooled infrared imager market comprises two primary types: passive infrared sensors and active infrared sensors. Passive infrared sensors detect thermal energy emitted by objects without emitting any energy themselves, making them ideal for applications like motion detection and surveillance. In contrast, active infrared sensors emit infrared radiation and analyze the reflection from objects, allowing for enhanced imaging capabilities in various conditions. Both types are vital in fields such as security, automotive, and thermal monitoring, driving innovation and market growth.</p></p>
