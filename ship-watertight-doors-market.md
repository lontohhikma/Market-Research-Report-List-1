Sliding watertight doors, on the other hand, move horizontally along tracks, optimizing space and facilitating quick operation in narrow areas. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing safety and maintaining structural integrity in vessels, catering to various vessel designs and operational requirements.</p></p>
