<p><p>The solar photovoltaic potting agent market is primarily divided into two types: one-component and dual-component potting agents. One-component potting agents are ready-to-use materials that offer ease of application and curing, commonly used for their efficiency in encapsulating solar cells. Dual-component potting agents consist of two separate components that must be mixed prior to application, allowing for customization in properties like viscosity and curing time. Both types enhance protection against environmental factors, ensuring durability and performance in solar energy systems.</p></p>
