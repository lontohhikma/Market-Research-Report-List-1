<p><p>The Aerospace Fluid Conveyance Systems market encompasses various components used for transporting fluids within aircraft. Hoses are flexible tubes designed to carry fluids under low to moderate pressure, while low-pressure ducts are rigid or semi-rigid structures handling fluid flow at lower pressures. High-pressure ducts are engineered to manage fluids at elevated pressures, often found in critical systems like fuel and hydraulic lines. Each type is vital for ensuring efficient and safe operation of aircraft systems during flight.</p></p>
