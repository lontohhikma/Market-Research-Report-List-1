<p><p>The rail grinding machine market is divided into two primary types: milling and grinding. Milling type machines utilize a rotary cutter to remove material from the rail surface, effectively reshaping and repairing rail profiles. They are efficient for large-scale rail maintenance. In contrast, grinding type machines employ abrasive wheels to smooth and refine the rail surface, enhancing track longevity and reducing noise. Both types play a crucial role in railway maintenance, ensuring safety and performance.</p></p>
