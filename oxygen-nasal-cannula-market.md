<p><p>The oxygen nasal cannula market is divided into two main types: low-flow and high-flow oxygen nasal cannulas. Low-flow oxygen nasal cannulas deliver supplemental oxygen at lower flow rates, typically ranging from 1 to 6 liters per minute, suitable for patients with mild respiratory issues. In contrast, high-flow oxygen nasal cannulas provide a higher flow rate, often exceeding 6 liters per minute, allowing for better oxygenation in patients with more severe respiratory conditions, enhancing comfort and treatment efficacy.</p></p>
