<p><p>The automotive whiplash protection device market focuses on two key components: backrests and head restraints. Backrests are designed to provide support and prevent excessive forward bending during a collision, reducing the risk of neck injury. Head restraints, positioned behind the head, limit rearward movement and stabilize the head upon impact. Both elements work together to mitigate the effects of whiplash, enhancing passenger safety and comfort in vehicles, while meeting regulatory requirements for crash protection technologies.</p></p>
