<p><p>Semiconductor package substrates in mobile devices encompass various types designed for efficient connectivity and compactness. MCP (Multi-Chip Package) integrates multiple chips, while UTCSP (Ultra Thin Chip Scale Package) offers a minimalist profile. FC-CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) enhances performance with direct chip-to-substrate connections. SiP (System in Package) consolidates diverse functions, PBGA (Plastic Ball Grid Array) and CSP (Chip Scale Package) offer balanced performance and footprint. BOC (Ball on Chip) and FMC (Flex Memory Card) cater to specialized applications, ensuring versatility in mobile technology.</p></p>
