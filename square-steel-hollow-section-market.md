<p><ul><li>Welded Steel Hollow Section</li><li>Seamless Steel Hollow Section</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The square steel hollow section market comprises two main types: welded and seamless steel hollow sections. Welded steel hollow sections are produced by welding together flat steel plates, offering cost-effectiveness and versatility for various applications. Seamless steel hollow sections are manufactured through extrusion or rotary piercing, providing higher strength and integrity without welds, making them suitable for high-stress applications. Both types find use in construction, infrastructure, and mechanical engineering, contributing to structural stability and efficiency.</p></p>
