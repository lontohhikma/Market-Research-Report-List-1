<p><strong>The Corn Silage Inoculants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterofermentative</li><li>Homofermentative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Corn silage inoculants are classified into two main types: heterofermentative and homofermentative. Heterofermentative inoculants promote the production of multiple fermentation products, such as organic acids and alcohols, enhancing the silage's aerobic stability and nutritional quality. These inoculants often improve the fermentation process under various conditions. In contrast, homofermentative inoculants primarily produce lactic acid, resulting in a rapid decline in pH and improved preservation of silage. Both types aim to optimize fermentation, enhance feed quality, and reduce spoilage during storage.</p></p>
