<p><strong>The Auto Parts Painting System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Coating Line</li><li>Liquid Coating Line</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto parts painting system market is divided into two main types: powder coating lines and liquid coating lines. Powder coating lines apply a dry powder that is electrostatically charged, providing a durable and environmentally friendly finish. This method offers excellent resistance to scratches and chemicals. In contrast, liquid coating lines use liquid paint applied through conventional spraying methods, offering a wider variety of colors and finishes but typically with a longer drying time. Each method serves distinct needs in automotive manufacturing.</p></p>
