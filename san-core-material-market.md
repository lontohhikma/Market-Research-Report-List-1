<p><p>The SAN Core Material market consists of two primary types: open molding and closed molding. Open molding involves processes like hand layup, where resin and fibers are layered in an open environment, allowing for easy manipulation and cost-effectiveness, but longer curing times. In contrast, closed molding utilizes techniques like resin transfer molding (RTM), where materials are enclosed, leading to enhanced finished quality, reduced waste, and faster production cycles. Both methods serve different applications in industries like automotive and marine manufacturing.</p></p>
