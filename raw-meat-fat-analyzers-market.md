<p><p>The Raw Meat Fat Analyzers market features two primary technologies: DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared Spectroscopy). DEXA technology utilizes X-ray radiation to assess fat distribution and composition, providing precise measurements of fat content in meat products. In contrast, NIR technology employs infrared light to analyze the molecular composition of meat, enabling rapid and non-destructive fat measurement. Both technologies are essential for quality control in the meat industry, enhancing product consistency and compliance with regulations.</p></p>
