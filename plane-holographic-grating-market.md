<p><p>The Plane Holographic Grating Market is categorized into two main types based on groove profiles: Sinusoidal Groove Profile and Shine Groove Profile. Sinusoidal Groove Profile gratings feature a smooth, continuous variation in groove depth, enabling efficient diffraction and superior optical performance. In contrast, Shine Groove Profile gratings have a distinct, step-like structure, leading to effective light manipulation and specialized applications. Both profiles play crucial roles in various optical systems, including spectroscopy and telecommunications, enhancing light control and signal processing capabilities.</p></p>
