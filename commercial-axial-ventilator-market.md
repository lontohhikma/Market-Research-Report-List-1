Single-phase ventilators typically operate on a single-phase power supply, making them suitable for smaller applications with lower power requirements. In contrast, three-phase ventilators utilize a three-phase power supply, offering greater efficiency and higher output, ideal for larger commercial spaces. Both types play crucial roles in ventilation systems, ensuring effective air circulation and maintaining indoor air quality across various settings.</p></p>
