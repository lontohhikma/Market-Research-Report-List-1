<p><p>Ceramic feedthrough capacitors are essential components used to provide electrical isolation and signal integrity in various applications. The market can be segmented into two primary types: plugins and patches. Plugin feedthrough capacitors are inserted directly into a circuit board and are ideal for high-density applications, offering efficient performance. In contrast, patch feedthrough capacitors are mounted on the surface of a circuit board, providing easier installation and space-saving benefits, making them suitable for compact electronic devices.</p></p>
