<p><p>Heat Interface Units (HIUs) are essential components in heating systems, facilitating efficient heat transfer between a central source and individual heating loads. The market for HIUs is primarily categorized into indirect and direct types. Indirect HIUs utilize a heat exchanger to transfer heat from the primary system to the secondary water circuit, providing flexibility and improved control. In contrast, direct HIUs transfer heat directly from the primary circuit to the end user's heating system, typically offering a more straightforward installation and lower costs.</p></p>
