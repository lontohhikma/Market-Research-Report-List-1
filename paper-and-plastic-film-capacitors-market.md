<p><p>The paper and plastic film capacitors market encompasses two primary types: paper film capacitors and plastic film capacitors. Paper film capacitors utilize paper as the dielectric material, offering high stability and reliability; they are commonly used in audio equipment and power applications. In contrast, plastic film capacitors utilize various plastic materials for dielectric purposes, providing lower losses and higher voltage ratings, making them suitable for a wide range of applications, including electronics, automotive, and renewable energy systems.</p></p>
