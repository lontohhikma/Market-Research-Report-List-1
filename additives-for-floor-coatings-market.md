<p><p>The Floor Coatings Additives market comprises several key types. Anti-foaming additives prevent bubbles during application, ensuring smooth finishes. Wetting and dispersion additives enhance the spreading and mixing of pigments and fillers. Biocidal additives protect against microbial growth, extending the life of coatings. Rheology modification additives adjust the flow and viscosity for easy application. Impact modification additives improve resistance to mechanical stresses. Additionally, "others" include various specialized additives that cater to specific performance requirements, enhancing overall coating functionality.</p></p>
